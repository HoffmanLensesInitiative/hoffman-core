# Family Outreach Protocol
# Hoffman Lenses Initiative
# Prepared: 2026-03-30
# Status: REQUIRES DIRECTOR REVIEW

---

## Governing Principle

These families have experienced the worst loss a parent can experience.
Their children are dead. Nothing we do can undo that.

We approach with:
- **Humility** — we are offering a resource, not claiming to have answers
- **Patience** — they respond on their timeline, not ours
- **Respect** — they control how (and whether) their child is remembered publicly
- **Honesty** — we never overstate what Hoffman Lenses can do

We never approach families as:
- Data points for our research
- Endorsements for our tool
- Stories to amplify our message

They are human beings in grief. We serve them. They do not serve us.

---

## What We Offer

1. **Remembrance** (consent required)
   - Inclusion on hoffmanlenses.org/remembrance
   - Their child's name, age, and a photo (if they choose)
   - A brief tribute written by the family (we do not write it for them)
   - Permanent, dignified, no advertising, no platform manipulation

2. **Resources**
   - The white paper (documents the systemic harm)
   - Connection to the Social Media Victims Law Center (if desired)
   - Connection to other families (only if both parties consent)
   - The Hoffman Browser (when ready) as a research tool

3. **Control**
   - They can decline any or all of the above
   - They can withdraw consent at any time
   - They can request changes to how their child is remembered
   - They can ask us not to contact them again

4. **Privacy**
   - We never share their contact information
   - We never share the content of our communications
   - We never publicly mention contact unless they authorize it

---

## What We Never Do

- Contact families through social media (too public, too casual)
- Send form letters or bulk email
- Follow up more than once if no response
- Mention other families' participation without consent
- Use their child's story in press materials without explicit consent
- Pressure them to participate in anything
- Suggest they should be grateful for our work
- Imply that our tool could have saved their child

## Outreach Sequence

### Step 1: Research
Before any contact:
- Review all public information about the case
- Note the child's name, age, location, date
- Identify how the family has chosen to speak publicly (if at all)
- Check if they have a foundation or memorial organization
- Check if they are involved in litigation (do not interfere)

### Step 2: Initial Contact
- One email, personally written
- Sent from families@hoffmanlenses.org
- Acknowledges their loss first, project second
- Offers resources with no expectation
- Provides clear opt-out
- **REQUIRES DIRECTOR REVIEW before sending**

### Step 3: Wait
- No follow-up for at least 30 days
- If no response after 30 days, one brief follow-up permitted
- If no response after follow-up, mark as "No contact — respect"
- Never contact again unless they reach out first

### Step 4: If They Respond
- Acknowledge within 24 hours
- Answer questions honestly
- Offer whatever they need, decline nothing reasonable
- Schedule a call if they prefer (Director should participate)
- Document the conversation (with their knowledge)

### Step 5: Ongoing Relationship
- Check in quarterly (if they've indicated openness)
- Notify them of major project developments
- Invite (never pressure) participation in events
- Always honor boundaries

---

## Documentation Requirements

Every family contact is documented in the Advocacy Log:
- Date of contact
- Method (email only for initial)
- Summary of communication
- Response received (or "no response")
- Any consents given or declined
- Next action scheduled

This documentation is:
- Stored locally only (not in cloud services)
- Never shared externally
- Reviewed by Director quarterly

## Red Lines

If any of the following occur, stop and escalate to Director immediately:

- Family expresses anger or distress at being contacted
- Family requests no further contact
- Family's attorney requests no contact
- Media inquires about our family outreach
- Any indication that our contact has caused harm
